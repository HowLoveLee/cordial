    • Registration Process


  • A: Log in, navigate to the Home Page, and click on the 'Book Now' button.


    From there, Select your Campus Location and Exam Category then choose an available Date and Time to complete registration.

  • A: Yes, you can register for up to three different exams. However, you cannot register for the same exam more than once on the same day.

  • A: You will receive a confirmation email shortly after completing your registration. This email includes the exam details, location, and additional instructions.

  • A: Once an exam time is full (20 seats are booked), it will no longer be available. You will need to select a different time or date.


    • Exam Day Information


  • A: Bring a valid photo ID and any required materials specified for your exam. Check your confirmation email for specific instructions.

  • A: The exam location, including the Building and Room Number, will be listed in your confirmation email. Please arrive at least 15 minutes early.


    • Rescheduling and Cancellations


  • A: Yes. You can reschedule your exam up to 24 hours before the scheduled time. Same-day rescheduling is not permitted.

  • A: To cancel your registration, log in, navigate the Home Page to 'Registration History', and select the exam you want to cancel. Confirm the cancellation to complete the process.


    • Technical Support and Faculty Access
